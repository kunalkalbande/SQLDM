Customer Support Portal

The Idera Customer Support Portal offers 24 x 7 worldwide support to all customers who hold a current maintenance contract with Idera and provides an easy and convenient way to:

  • Submit or track technical support cases.
  • Search the Idera Knowledge Base.
  • Download the latest version of your Idera products.
  • Access information about your support plan and licenses.
  • Access Idera resources such as the community site, blog, product videos, and white papers.

Access the Idera Customer Support Portal

Idera SQL Customers click this link to enter the portal: https://idera.secure.force.com/

For any questions regarding access to the Customer Support Portal, contact your Idera Sales representative.
